OUR IMPACT

What do we align with?

Drop, a revolutionary water company, is committed to making a significant impact on the bottled water industry by taking a unique approach. Unlike conventional brands solely focused on maximizing profits, Drop allocates an admirable 50% of its earnings towards combating the dominance of big water brands. This bold initiative reflects Drop's dedication to addressing environmental concerns, promoting sustainability, and ensuring access to clean water for communities worldwide. By redirecting a substantial portion of its profits towards these crucial causes, Drop not only distinguishes itself in the market but also contributes to a more sustainable and equitable future for all.
